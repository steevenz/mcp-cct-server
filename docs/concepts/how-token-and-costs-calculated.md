# 💸 How Tokens and Costs are Calculated

Welcome to the **CCT Financial Transparency Layer**. This guide explains how we track your "Cognitive Investment" in real-time.

## 1. The Token Economy 🪙
Every time you interact with an AI model, data is exchanged in units called **Tokens**.
- **Input Tokens**: The instructions and context you send to the AI.
- **Output Tokens**: The response and thoughts generated by the AI.

CCT uses a high-precision tokenizer to ensure that your usage is measured down to the single token.

## 2. High-Precision Cost Tracking 📈
CCT doesn't just guess your costs—it performs real-time financial audits for every cognitive step.

- **USD Calculation**: We use the latest pricing datasets from `database/datasets/`. Most modern models charge per 1 million tokens. We calculate this with **8 decimal points** of precision.
- **IDR Conversion**: Since exchange rates fluctuate, CCT uses a **Live Forex Service** to convert USD to IDR at the moment of the calculation. This ensures your IDR balance is always grounded in current market reality.

## 3. The "Pessimistic" Safety Net (ai-common-model) 🛡️
Sometimes, an AI model might be so new that we don't have its exact pricing yet. 

To protect you from "billing shock," we use the **Pessimistic Cost Averaging** method:
- We identify the most powerful **Premium** models (like Claude 3.5 Opus or GPT-4o).
- We calculate their **Highest Average** price.
- We apply this average as a fallback (under the name `ai-common-model`).

> [!TIP]
> **Why do we do this?** 
> It's better to estimate a cost that is slightly higher than reality than to tell you it's free. When the exact pricing is found, the system will automatically switch to the more accurate (and often cheaper) rate.

## 4. Where can I see my costs? 🖥️
- **In-Chat**: After every `think` step, the AI will report the cost of that specific internal debate.
- **Status Dashboard**: Visit `http://localhost:8000/status` to see your lifetime token usage and total cost in both USD and IDR.
