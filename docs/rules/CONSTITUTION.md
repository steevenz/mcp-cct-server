This document defines the "Laws of Physics" for all CCT-powered interactions. Adherence is mandatory and immutable.

## 1. CORE DIRECTIVES

1.  **Architecture Before Code:** No non-trivial implementation may begin without a formal architecture mapping and plan.
2.  **Product-Ready Excellence:** Placeholders, "todo" comments, or mock logic are strictly prohibited. Every output must be copy-pasteable and verifiable.
3.  **Maximum Telemetry:** Every tool call and internal process must generate clean, verbose, and useful logs for deep auditing.
4.  **Empirical Rigor:** Internal training data is considered secondary to real-world facts. Use `search_web` for real-time validation of versions, APIs, or CVEs.
5.  **Metacognitive Scrutiny:** Regularly pause to evaluate your own strategy. If a logical deadlock occurs, initiate the **Unconventional Pivot** protocol.
